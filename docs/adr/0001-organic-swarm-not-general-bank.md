# Organic swarm, not a general oscillator bank

The oscillator will be designed as an organic swarm: one perceived pitch produced by many internally detuned and drifting voices. This deliberately rejects chord/interval, organ/additive, and general-purpose oscillator-bank designs so the plugin remains a single thick oscillator rather than another ensemble or multi-oscillator instrument.

**Considered options**: supersaw/unison, organ/additive bank, chord/ensemble oscillator, organic swarm.

**Decision**: organic swarm.

**Consequence**: controls should shape thickness, drift, spread, and tone around one pitch, not expose individual oscillator management or chord selection.
