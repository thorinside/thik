# Medium swarm that remains pitched

The oscillator will use a medium swarm: thick enough to sound like a bank of oscillators, but constrained enough to preserve a clear perceived pitch. The design should be organic and alive without becoming an unpitched cloud or massive ensemble wash.

**Considered options**: subtle drift, medium swarm, huge swarm, density control, fixed huge sound.

**Decision**: medium swarm.

**Consequence**: voice count, detune range, drift depth, and stereo spread should be limited so the oscillator remains musically pitched.
